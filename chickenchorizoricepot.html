Chicken and Chorizo Paella
Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion sliced
  • 2 small red peppers, deseeded and sliced
  • 50g chorizo thinly sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves crushed
  • 1 red chille chopped (deseeded if you dont like it too hot)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 250g long grain rice
  • 1 large or 2 small chicken breasts diced
  • 100g frozen peas
  • zest and juice of 1 lemon

Method
  1. Boil the kettle, Heat the oil in a shallow pan with a lid, add the chicken, onion, peppers, chorizo, garlic and chilli, then fry over high heat for 3 mins. Add the turmeric and rice, stirring to ensure rice is coated. Pour in 500ml boiling water cover and cook for 12 mins
  2. Uncover and then stir, the rice should be almost tender, stir in the peas, with a splash more water if the rice is looking dry, then cook for 1 min more, until the rice is tender
  3. Stir in the lemon zest and juice with seasoning
Serve
  • Serve with Lemon wedges
