Toffee Sponge Puddings with Toffee Sauce
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • 175g Self Raising Flour
  • 115g Softened Butter
  • 115g Dark brown Soft Sugar
  • 2 Medium Eggs
  • Walnuts to serve

    • For the sauce

  • 50g Softened Butter
  • 25g Soft Dark Brown Sugar
  • 2 Tbsp Double Cream

    • Method
    1. Preheat Oven to 200 C (180 Fan), Grease four 175ml Pudding basins and line bases with greasproof paper
    2. Beat the flour, butter, sugar and eggs together until the mixtuire is smooth, spoon into the basins and bake for 20 mins until springy to the touch
    3. To make the sauce, heat the butter and sugar and cream until the sugar disolves. Boil for appoximately 1 min until it thickens
    4. Turn the puddings out into individual dishes and spoon the sauce over each Oven Top with walnut peices and serve with cream custard or ice cream
