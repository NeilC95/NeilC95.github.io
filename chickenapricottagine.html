Chicken Apricot Tagine
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • 1 Tbsp Veg Oil
  • 500g Chicken thigh fillets, roughly chopped
  • 2 Onions, finely sliced
  • 1 Garlic Clove, crushed
  • 2.5 cm Fresh Root Ginger, peeled and grated
  • 1 Tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 1/2 Tsp GHround Cinnamon
  • 400g tin Chopped Tomatoes
  • 250ml Chicken Stock
  • 100g ready to eat dried Apricots, halved
  • 1 Tbsp runny Honey
  • 100g Giant Couscous
  • Large handful of Coriander, roughly chopped
  • 50g Flaked Almonds
  • Flatbread and Yoghurt to serve

    • Method
    1. In a large pan, heat the oil over medium heat and gently fry Onions until soft, add the garlig, ginger and spices and cook for 1 min until fragrant
    2. Add the chopped chicken thighs to the pan and fry until golden, about 5 mins
    3. Add the tomato, stock, apricots and honey, bring to the boil and then turn down to simmer, cover the pan with a lid and cook for 30 mins stirring occasionally
    4. Stir in couscous and simmer for 6 mins, uncovered, until couscous is tender, check seasoning, scatter with almods and coriander, serve with flatbread and yoghurt.
