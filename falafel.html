Falafel
Ingredients

  • 400g tin of chickpeas rinsed and drained
  • 1 small red onion
  • 1 Garlic clove
  • 1 handful of parsley
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp harissa paste or chilli powder
  • 2 Tbsp Plain flour
  • 2 Tbsp Sunflower oil

Prep
  1. Chop Onion in to chunks
  2. Finely chop garlic
Method
  1. Drain can of chickpeas and pat dry with kitchen paper
  2. Tip into food processor along with chopped onion, garlic parsley, cumim, coriander, harissa or chilli powder, plain flour and a little salt
  3. Blend until fairly smooth, then shape into four patties with your hands
  4. Heat 2 tbsp of oil in a non stick frying pan, add the burgers. then quickly fry for 3 mins on each side until lightly golder brown
Serve
  • Serve with Toasted Pitta bread, salsa and green salad
