Easy Tomato soup
Ingredients

    Shopping List

  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Tbsp Unsalted Butter
  • 1 Large onion cut into pieces
  • 1 Clove Garlic
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp Plain flour to thicken
  • 2 (400g) tins of chopped tomatoes
  • 750ml chicken stock
  • 1 Tbsp Sugar to taste
  • Salt and Black pepper to taste
  • 3 Tbsp freshly chopped chives

Prep
  1. Chop Onion in to chunks
  2. crush garlic
Method
  1. Heat oil and butter in pan, add Onion and stir until softened, about 5 mins, stir in garlic and 1 1/2 tbsp of plain flour
  2. Add Tomatoes and chicken stock, bring to the boil, Simmer for 15 mins Season to taste with sugar salt and pepper
  3. Puree with stick blender
  4. Garnish with finely chopped chives and serve
