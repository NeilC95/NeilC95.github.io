Ingredients

    Tyga Pots

    • Ginger and Garlic Paste
    • Tyga Masala No.7
    • Kaskmiri Chilli Mix

    Shopping List

  • 1 Onion
  • 300g Mushrooms
  • 3 tsp Vegtable Oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2tsp tomato puree

Prep
  1. Finely dice the onions
  2. Finely slice the mushrooms
Method
  1. Gently heat the oil, add the onion and garlic and ginger paste, fry for 4 minutes stirring continuously
  2. Add Tyga Masala No. 7, 1 tsp Kashmiri chilli and salt, fry for 5 minutes
  3. Add tomato puree and 100ml water and fry for further 5 minutes
  4. Add the mushrooms, cover with lid and cook for 5-10 minutes
