Chinese Spare Ribs
Ingredients

    Shopping List

  • 12 meaty pork ribs
  • 100mls hiosin sauce
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp clear honey
  • 1 tbsp vineger
  • 1 tsp chinese 5 spice

Method
  1. Put the ribs into a large pan and cover with water, bring to the boil ans simmer for 15 mins
  2. Meanwhile pour the hoisin sauce into a jug and stir in the say sauce honey vinegar and five spice
  3. Drain the ribs very well, then brush with glaze
  4. Heat the grill to a high heat and grill for 15/20 mins until shiny and slightly charred
