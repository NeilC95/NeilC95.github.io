Strawberry and Lemon Thyme Scones
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • 250g Self raising flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 50g butter cubed and chilled
  • 25g caster sugar plus a little extra for sprinkling
  • 150ml Milk
  • 7g packed freeze dried strawberries, or 75g fresh chopped strawberries
  • sprigs of lemon thyme, leaves picked
  • 1 Egg beaten

    • Method
    1. Heat the oven to 220 C
    2. Rub together the flour baking powder and butter until you have a breadcrumb like consistency
    3. Add the sugar, milk, strawberries and thyme leaves and roughly stir with the blade of a knifeuntil the mixture just comes together
    4. Tip the mixture onto a lightly floured work surface and roll out to 2.5cm thickness, using a 6cm cutter, cut out chapes and arrange on a baking sheet
    5. Brush with beaten egg and sprinkle a little sugar on top
    6. Bake for 15 mins until golden
