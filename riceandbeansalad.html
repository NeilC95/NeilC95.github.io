Rice and Bean Salad
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • Basmati and Wild Rice
  • Can of Mixed Beans and Chickpeas
  • Spring Onion
  • Tomatoe
  • Chilli
  • Coriander
  • Lemon
  • Feta Cheese

    • Method
    1. Cook rice, adding beans and chickpeas for final 2 mins
    2. Drain and transfer to large bowl
    3. Add sliced spring onions and sliced tomatoe, finely sliced chilli and chopped coriander
    4. Stir well, drizzle in lemon juice, and crumble over feta cheese
    5. Season to taste and serve with lemon wedges
