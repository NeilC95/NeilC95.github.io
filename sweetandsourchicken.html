Sweet and Sour Chicken
Ingredients

    Sweet and Sour Chicken

    • 1/2 small pineapple (or tinned pineapple in juice)
    • 2 boneless chicken breasts
    • 1 tbsp veg oil
    • 1 tbsp sesame seed oil
    • 1 onion
    • 1 pepper
    • 1 tbsp cornflour
    • 4 spring onions

    For the sauce

  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 300 ml pineapple juice or juice from tin made up with water
  • 2 garlic cloves crushed
  • 35g fresh root ginger grated
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp white wine vineger
  • 1 tbsp soft brown sugar
  • 3 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 pinch dried chilli flakes
  • freshly ground pepper

Prep
  1. Cut onions into wedges
  2. de-seed and chop peppers
  3. trim and finely diagonally slice spring onions
Method
  1. To make sauce, pue the cornflour in a small bowl and stir in two tbsp of juice until it is smooth, Put the remainder of the pineapple juice into a separate bowl and stir in the garlic, ginger, soy, vineger, sugar, ketchup and chilli flakes until combined, set to one side
  2. cut pineapple into chunks, and cut chicken in to small diced chunks
  3. Heat the oil in a large pan/wok and stir fry the onions and peppers for 3 mins over a high heat, Coat the chicken in the cornflour and add to the pan. Stir fry for 4 mins until very lightly coloured on all sides. Add the pineapple and sweet and sour sauce to the pan with the chicken and bring to the simmer over medium heat, cook for 4-5 mins stirring regularly, until the chicken is cooked through
  4. Stir in the cornflour mix and cook for 30/60 seconds until thickened and gloss, turning the chicken until nicely coated
Serve
  • sprinkle with chopped spring onions
