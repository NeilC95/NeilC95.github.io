Enriched Chelsea Buns
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • 500 g flour
  • 350g milk (scalded at 80 C then cooled to 40 C)
  • 1 Egg
  • 80g Sugar
  • 5g fresh yeast or 1 tsp, dissolved in a little water
  • 75g butter

    • Filling

  • Melted butter
  • Cinnamon
  • Sugar
  • Ground Roasted Hazelnuts
  • Egg wash

    • Method
    1. First mix and start to knead all the Ingredients for the dough except the butter Develope the gluten
    2. Then add the butter, melted if you prefer and incorporate completeley
    3. Bulk rise overnight
    4. Next morning ... Take dough from bowl and place on floured surface, making rectangle 36 x 30 cm
    5. Smear with melted butter, shake on sugar and cinnamon
    6. Roll up, Seal and cut into 12 slices, place on 2 trays, press slices to flatten top bruck with egg wash and sprinkle sugar over the top /li>
    7. Cover and leave to rise 30-60 mins
    8. Bake in oven at 180 deg C for 20 mins or until golden
