Chinese Rice
Ingredients

Shopping List

  • 1-2 spring onions
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1.3 kg cold cooked rice
  • 1-2 tsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • pepper to taste
  • 4 tbsp oil for stir frying

    • Prep
    1. Wash and finely chop the spring onion
    2. Lightly beat eggs and add salt and pepper
    Method
    1. Heat wok or large frying pan and add 2 tbsp oil
    2. When oil is hot add the eggs, cook stirring until they are lightly scrambled. Remove the eggs and clean pan
    3. Add 2 tbsp oil, add the rice and stir fry for a few minutes, using chop sticks or wooden spoon to break them apart
    4. Stir in soy sauce
    5. When rice is heated through, add the scrambled egg back into the pan, stir in the spring onions and serve
