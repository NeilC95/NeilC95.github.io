Coconut and lime Chicken
Ingredients

  • 8 skinless chicken thighs
  • zest and juice of 2 limes, plus wedges to serve
  • 2 tsp meduim curry powder or garam masala
  • 1 tsp chilli powder (optional)
  • 50g desiccated coconut
  • 1tbsp vegetable oil

Method
  1. Preheat oven to 200C/180C fan
  2. Put the chicken in a large bowl with the lime zest and juice, curry powder, chilli powder (if using) and seasoning
  3. Mix well then toss in the coconut
  4. place chicken on racksitting in a roasting tin, drizzle with the oil then bake for 25 minutes until cooked through and tender
Serve
  • Serve with Mango chutney, Lime wedges for squeezing over and rice
