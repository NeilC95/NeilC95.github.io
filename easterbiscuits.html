Classic Easter Biscuits
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • 225g SR flour
  • 115g butter
  • 115g sugar
  • 55g currants
  • 1 egg

    • Method
    1. Set the oven to 170ÂºC.
    2. Rub the butter into the flour.
    3. Add the sugar and currants.
    4. Stir in the beaten egg and form into a ball.
    5. Roll out on a floured surface and use a fluted cutter to cut out the biscuits.
    6. Place on a floured baking tray and bake for around 12-15 minutes until golden brown. Be careful not to overbake as they burn very easily.
