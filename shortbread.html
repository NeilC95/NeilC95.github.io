Buttery Shortbread Biscuits
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • 225g Plain white flour plus extra for dusting
  • 225g unsalted Butter, softened, plus extra for greasing
  • 110g Caster Sugar plus extra for dusting
  • 110g Cornflour
  • Pinch of Salt

    • Method
    1. Lightly butter 2 baking trays, or line with baking parchment
    2. Put butter and suger into large bowl and cream together, using an electric hand wisk or wooden spoon, until light and fluffy, sift the flour and cornflour into the bowladd the salt and mix together until smoothly combined Tip the mixture out on to lightly floured surface and knead to a soft dough
    3. Roll out the dough between 2 pieces of baking parchment to a thickness of 1cm, prick the dough all over with a fork and cut into triangles or what ever shape you prefer, re-roll the scraps to make more (if you re-roll to many times they the dough may start to become greasy)
    4. Put the shortbreads on the prepared tray and chill for at least 30 mins
    5. Heat your oven to 170 C
    6. Bake the shortbreads for about 20 mins, until just turning golden brown at the edges, leave on the baking tray for a few mins to firm up slightly then lift the shortbreads on to wire rack, dust with sugar, they will keep in an airtight container for 3-4 days
