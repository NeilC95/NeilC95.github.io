Crispy Seaweed
Ingredients

    Shopping List

  • 1kg spring greens
  • 600mls Veg oil
  • 1 1/2 tsp caster sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 50g flaked almonds

Prep
  1. Shave pring greens into very fine shreds
  2. Wash greens and dry, spreading out on to kitchen towel for at least 30 mins
Method
  1. Heat oil until smoke point, remove from heat and add greens stri fry for 2.5 mins remove and drain
  2. Arrange in serving dish, sprinkle with sugar, salt and flacked almonds
