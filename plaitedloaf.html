Plaited Loaf
Ingredients

Shopping List

  • 330g plain flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp dried yeast
  • 1 tsp milk
  • 25g butter-margerine
  • 1 egg
  • a little milk or egg whilte for glazing
  • 2 tbsp poppy seeds

    • Method
    1. Mix together the flour, sugar, salt and yeast. warm the milk and butter in the microwave for 30 seconds, then mix into the flour with the egg and knead to a stiff dough. Knead until elastic and no longer sticky. Place in an oiled bowl, cover with oiled clingfilm and leave in warm place for about an hour until doubled in size.
    2. Knead again and shape into three sausage shapes about 20cm long. Moisten and seal one end of each strip and press them together, then plait them together, moisten and seal the ends Place on greased baking sheet, cover with oiled clingfilm and leave to rise for about 40 mins until doubled in size.
    3. Brush with milk or egg white and sprinkle with poppy seeds. Bake in pre-heated over at 190 deg Fan, for about 20-25 mins until golden brown
