White Loaf
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • 500g strong bread flour plus some for dusting
  • 14g yeast
  • 1 1/2 tsp olive oil
  • 10g salt
  • 1 Egg

    • Method
    1. Place flour in bowl, add yeast one side and salt the other and mix
    2. Add a good splash of olive oil, measure out 340ml of water and add three quarters to the flour mixture, mix by hand and then add the rest
    3. Turn the dough out, and knead by hand until the dough looks silky and smooth place in oiled bowl and cover leaev to rise until doubled in size, 1 hour
    4. when risen knock back and shape into the shape you want your bread
    5. Prove for another hour, pre-heat oven to 200 deg C
    6. Brush loaf with beaten egg and bake for 20-30 mins until golden
