Soda Bread
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • 350g plain flour plus some for dusting
  • 150g wholemeal flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1 tsp table salt
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 450ml buttermilk

    • Method
    1. pre-heat the oven to 200 deg C, Sift the dry ingredients into a bowl and make a well in the centre, reserve 2 tablespoons of the buttermilk and add the rest to the bowl, mixing lightly with a fork as you add it (be careful not to overwork the dough but make sure all the dry ingredients are mixed in, Add the remaining butter milk if necessary)
    2. Tip the dough on to a floured surfaceand knead gently for 30 seconds to combine, Do not Overwork, line an baking sheet with baking paper and dust with flour, Form the bread into a round, place on a rtay and flatten slightly, use a serrated knife to cut a deep cross in the top of the loaf
    3. Place into oven and bake for 30-35 mins until the Soda bread is golden on the outside and cooked through, A good way to check is by tapping the base - it should sound hollow when cooked
    4. Cool on wire rack and enjoy cool or warm
