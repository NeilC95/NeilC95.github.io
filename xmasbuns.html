Christmas (Chelsea) Buns
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • 500g Strong white flour plus extra for dusting
  • 10g Salt
  • 10g fast acting yeast
  • 300ml Full Fat Milk
  • 40g Unsalted Butter
  • 1 Medium Egg, beaten

    • For the filling

  • 25g unsalted Butter
  • 75g soft brown Sugar
  • 100g Dried Cranberries
  • 100g Ready to eat dries Apricots, chopped
  • 2 Tsp Cinnamon

    • For the Glaze

  • 75g Apricot jam

    • For the Icing

  • 100g Icing Sugar
  • Finely grated zest of 1/2 lemon
  • 1 Tbsp Water

    • Method
    1. Warm the milk and butter in a saucepanuntil the butter is melted and the mixture is lukewarm
    2. Tip the flour into a large mixing bowl, add the salt to one side and the yeast to the other, add the milk mixture andthe egg and stir together with your hands to make a rough dough, you may need to add a little more flour
    3. Tip the dough on to a generously floured work surface and begin to knead keep kneading for 5-10 mins, work through the wet stage until the dough starts to form a soft smooth skin
    4. When your dough feels smooth and silky, put it into a large lightly oiled bowl, cover with a tea towel and leave to rise until doubled in size, at least one hour but fine to leave it for 2 or even 3 hours
    5. Line a deep sided backing tray with baking parchment or silicon paper
    6. Tip the dough out ontio a lightly floured work surface, without knocking it back, roll it into a rectangle approx 5mm thick, tack down the edge closest to you, brush the surface all over with melted butter, then sprinkle with brown sugar, cinnamon and dried fruit, roll the dough up into a tight cylinder and cut into 9 slices, place these cut side up into your baking tray leaving a little space between each one
    7. Cover the dough with a tea towel and set aside to rise for 30 mins, meanwhile heat your oven to 190 C
    8. Bake the buns for 20-25 mins or until risen and golden, warm the apricot jam with a splash of water, sieve and brush over the hot buns to glaze, then set aside on wire rack to cool
    9. When the buns are cool, mix the ingredients for the lemon icing together to make smooth icing and trickle across the top of the buns
