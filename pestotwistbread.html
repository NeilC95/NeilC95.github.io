Pesto Twist Bread
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • 500g strong white flour
  • 10g dried yeast
  • 10g salt
  • 300g water
  • 3 tbsp pesto

    • Method
    1. Make the dough by weighing flour into large mixing bowl and then adding yeast to one side and salt to the other, make a well and pour water into the well.
    2. Using your hands form a dough, when the dough has formed tip out on to counter
    3. Knead for 10 mins until smooth and elastic, place in bowl cover and leave to rise until doubled in size
    4. Heat oven to 220 deg C, Knock back dough, and roll to rectangle 45 x 30 cm approx
    5. Evenly spread pesto over the top, leaving 2 cm border round the edges, roll the long side towards you and pinch the seam closed, cut the dough in half down the length of the dough, working quickly plait/twist the two peices, trying to keep the pieces twisted so that the cut end remains on top.
    6. Cover and leave to prove for 30 mins
    7. Bake in oven for 25-30 mins or until golden
