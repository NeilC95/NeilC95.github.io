Chicken Fajita Pasta Bake
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • 275g Amori Pasta
  • 500g Chicken thigh fillets, cut into strips
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Onion, finely sliced
  • 30g pack of Fajita seasoning mix
  • 3 mixed Peppers, deseeded and sliced
  • 1 Tbsp Tomato puree
  • 2 x 200g tubs of fresh Tomato Salsa
  • 75g grated Cheddar cheese
  • Soured cream to serve (optional)

    • Method
    1. Bring a large pan of salted water to the boil and cook pasta according to the pack instructions, until al dente, Drain.
    2. Meanwhile heat the oil in a large frying pan medium-high heat, fry the chicken strips for 5 min, until giolden and cooked through, lift onto plate with slotted spoon.
    3. Add the onion and peppers into the empty pan and fry over medium heat until softened and beginning to caramelise, about 8 mins, add Fajita seasoning mix, fry for 1 min the return the chicken to the pan with tomato puree, salsa and 50ml water, simmer for a couple of mins, then add drained pasta and check for seasoning
    4. Pre-heat grill to medium, spoon pasta into a 2 litre ovenproof dish, sprinkle over the cheese and grill until bubbling and golden, serve with a dollop of sour cream
