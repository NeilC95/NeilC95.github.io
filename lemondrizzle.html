Lemon Drizzle Cake
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • 170g Self raising flour
  • 170g Caster sugar
  • 170 g Margarine plus extra for greasing
  • 2 Medium Eggs
  • 3 unwaxed Lemons
  • 4 tbsp Icing sugar

    • Method
    1. Pre-heat oven to 180 deg C, cream the butter and sugar together
    2. Add both eggs and whisk in
    3. Sift in the flour and fold until smooth
    4. Add the zest of two lemons and the juice of one to the mixture and mix well
    5. Spoon the mixture into a greased and lightly floured cake tin (9 inch springform tin recommended)
    6. Bake for 25-30 mins, the cake should be golden on top and skewer should come out clean
    7. In a small pan, mix the joice of two lemons with the icing sugar, heat over medium heat and stir until you have a syrupy texture
    8. Poke holes all over the cake with skewer, then take the syrup off the heat and pour over the cooling sponge
    9. Allow to cool and then enjoy a slice with a nice cup of tea
