Ingredients

    Tyga Pots

    • Tyga Masala No.20
    • Kashmiri Chilli

    Shopping List

  • 1 Onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 250g brussel sprouts/green cabbage/brocolli
  • 2 tsp Vegtable Oil
  • 1 tsp salt

Prep
  1. Finely dice the onion
  2. Finely slice the garlic
  3. Curt the brussel sprouts in half or roughly chop the green veg
Method
  1. Bring a pan of water to the boil and parboil the sprouts for 4 minutes then drain
  2. In a frying pan over a high heat, add the onions, garlis and salt ans stir continuously for 4-5 mins
  3. Add Tyga masala no. 20 and Kashmiri chilli (1/4 tsp for mild, 1/2 tsp for medium and 1 tsp for hot) stir for 1 minute
  4. Add the sprouts and cover with a lid, cook for 3-5 minutes or until sprouts are cooked through
