Focacia
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • 500g strong white bread flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 sachets easy blend yeast
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 400ml cold water
  • oilive oil for drizzling
  • fine sea salt

    • Method
    1. Place flour, salt, yeast, olive oil and 300ml of the water in large bowl, gently stir with yor hand to form dough, then knead the dough in the bowl for 5 mins, gradually adding the remaining water
    2. Stretch the dough by hand in the bowl, tuck the sides in to the centre, turn the bowl 80 degrees and repeat the process for 5 mins
    3. Tip the dough on to oiled work surface and continue to knead for 5 more mins, return to the bowl cover and leave to rise until double in size
    4. Line two large baking sheets with greaseproof paper, tip the dough out of the bowl and devide into two portion, flatten each portion onto the baking tray pushing to the corners and leave to prove for 1 hour
    5. pre-heat the oven to 220 deg C, drizzle the loaves with olive oil, sprinkle with sea salt then bake foer 20 mins, when cooked drizzle a little more olive oil and serve warm or hot
