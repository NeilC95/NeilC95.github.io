Chow Mein
Ingredients

    Shopping List

  • 450g egg noodles
  • 250g pork fillet shredded
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 5 tsp Vegtable Oil
  • 100g canned bamboo shoots shredded
  • 1/2 cucumber shredded
  • 100g spinach leaves shredded

    • Sauce

  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp chinese wine or dry sherry
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 1 tsp sesame oil

Method
  1. Plunge the noodles into pan of boiling water and simmer for 10 mins or until soft but not sticky, drain and refresh
  2. Toss the shredded pork in cornflour
  3. Heat about half of the oil in a wok or frying pan, place the noodles in a large bowl tossing with fork to seperate them then pour over the hot oil, Stir to ensure noodles are evenly coated Return the noodles to the wok and stir fry for 2-3 mins, Remove the noodles with a slotted spoon, and place in a serving dish
  4. Heat the remaining oil in the wok and quickly stir fry the pork, bamboo shoots, cucumber and spinach for 2 mins, then pour the mixture over the noodles, sprinkle with sesame oil and serve
