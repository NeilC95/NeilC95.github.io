Ingredients

    Tyga Pots

    • Ginger and Garlic Paste
    • Tyga Masala No.5
    • Tyga Masala No.2
    • Tyga Masala No.6
    • Methi Sachet
    • Kashmiri Chilli

    Shopping List

  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves
  • 4 tbsp Vegtable Oil
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 750g Chicken
  • 300g Rice

Prep
  1. Finely dice the onions
  2. Finely chop one tomato and cut the other into 8 peices
  3. Chop the Chicken into bite size pieces
  4. Finely chop coriander keeping them seperate
  5. If you dislike while spices, grind the 5 x clove and 5 x cardamom from the Tyga Masala No. 5 in and pestle and mortar
Method
  1. Heat 3 tbsp of oil in a frying pan over a high heat and add the onions, ginger and garlic paste, Tyge masala no.6, Methi leaves, finely chopped tomato, ground or whole spices from Tyga masala no.5 and 1tsp salt, stir continuously for 4 mins
  2. reduce to medium heat and add Tyga masala no. 2, tomato puree and kashmiri chilli (1 tsp for mild, 2 for medium or 3 for hot) stir continuously for 30 seconds
  3. Add 300ml water and simmer for 2 minutes stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the chicken, cover with lid and simmer for a further 15 minutes, stirring occasionally until the chicken is cooked
  5. add the pieces of tomato, cover and cook for further 5 minutes
  6. sprinkle with corander leaves
Rice
  • Add 1/2 pint of rice to 1/2 pint of cold water, cover and bring to the boil and simmer, still covered for 3 minutes, remove from the heat and leave covered for 45 minuted for the perfect rice
Serve
  • Serve with rice and Bombay Potato
