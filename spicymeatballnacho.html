Spicy Meatball Nachos
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • Splash Worcestershire Sauce
  • 400g Can Kidney Beans, Drained And Rinsed
  • 700g Bottle Passata
  • 12 Rashers Smoked Streaky Bacon, Chopped
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1/2 Tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1/2 Tsp Hot Chilli Powder
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, Crushed
  • 500g Beef Mince
  • 300g Tortilla Chips
  • 100g Mozzarella, Torn
  • 100g Medium Cheddar, Grated
  • 2 Tbsp Jalapenos, Drained

    • Method
    1. heat oven to Mark 6/200c. in a bowl, mix together mince, garlic, chilli, cumin and a pinch of salt. shape into 30-40 small meatballs. heat a non stick frying pan until very hot, fry the meatballs in batches for about 5mins, turning, until browned. transfer to a plate.
    2. add oil to the pan and fry onion and bacon for 5-10mins until golden. add the passata, beans and worcestershire sauce, simmer for 10mins. add meatballs and simmer for 10mins, stirring occasionally.
    3. put the tortilla chips into an ovenproof dish, bake for 5mins, then spoon over meatballs and sauce. sprinkle over cheese and jalapenos. bake for 2-3mins, until the cheese has melted. serve straightaway.
