Pecan Brownies
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • 380g unsalted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 380g dark chocolate (70% cocoa), chopped
  • 380g caster sugar
  • 200g plain fl;our
  • 2 small pinches Cinnamon
  • 150g pecans, roughly chopped
  • 6 Eggs
  • salted caramel ice cream and caramel sauce to serve

    • Method
    1. Pre-heart oven to 180 C, grease and line a 24cm x 30cm brownie tin
    2. Melt the butter and dark chocolate in a large heatproof bowl sitting over a pan of water on low heat and being careful not to burn the chocolate, stir in the sugar until it dissolves
    3. Remove the bowl from the heat and sift in the flour, cinnamon and a good pinch of salt, add the pecans and stir to combine
    4. Beat the eggs in a jug and then slowly add to the chocolate mixture mixing constantly until fully incorporated
    5. Pour into the tin and bake for 30-35 mins, until the outside is dark and the top is beginning to crack
    6. Leave to cool in the tin for 15 mins before turning out on to a wire rack, leave to ccol for 15-29 mins before serving with ice cream etc
