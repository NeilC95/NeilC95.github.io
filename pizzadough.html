Pizza Dough
Ingredients - 2 Pizza bases

Shopping list

  • 250 g plain flour
  • 4g sugar
  • 4g dried yeast
  • 2g salt
  • 150ml water
  • 2 tsp olive oil

    • Method
    1. dissolve the dried yeast and usgar in a bit of your lukewarm water and stir well, let the yeast sit for 20 minutes
    2. Sift the flour, add the olive oil and the salt to it, and then the yeast and the rest of the water
    3. Knead well, this takes 10-15 mins, then let the dough sit for 2 hours to prove
    4. Knead again roll out and it is ready to use
    5. Bake in oven at Maximum temperature, 350 deg C
