Katsu Curry
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • 2 Breaded Chicken fillets
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Large Carrots, chopped and one peeled into ribbons
  • 2 Large Garlic cloves, crushed
  • Thumb Size Peice of Ginger, peeled and grated, or finely chopped
  • 1 Tbsp Curry powder, mild or medium
  • 1/2 Tsp Turmeric
  • 400ml Can Coconut Milk
  • 2 Tsp Maple Syrup

    • Method
    1. Preheat the oven and cook the breaded chicken as per pack instructions, meanwhile...
    2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan, cook the onions and chopped carrots until the onions are soft and starting to caramelise, about 8 mins.
    3. Add the garlic and ginger and sizzle for another 30 secs, then stir in the curry powder and turmeric.
    4. Once the spices are warmed through, add the coconut milk, maple syrup or honey and 100ml water.
    5. Season well, cover and simmer over a low heat for 20 mins.
    6. When the onions and carrots in the curry sauce are soft, blitz using a hand or table-top blender.
    7. If the sauce is too thick, add a little more water, check the seasoning, adding more salt, maple syrup or some lime juice, if you like. Keep warm.
    8. Cut and arrange chicken on a plate, pour over one end some of the Katsu Curry sauce and add some rice, decorate with carrot ribbons
