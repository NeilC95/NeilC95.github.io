Tarragon Chicken
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • 4 x 125g skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite size pieces
  • 25g plain flour
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 250g Chestnut Mushrooms, sliced
  • 150ml white wine
  • 300g Creme Fraiche
  • 1/2 Tsp Dijon Mustard
  • 2 TbspFresh Tarragon, chopped
  • Large handful of Spinach
  • Crusty Bread to serve (optional)

    • Method
    1. Put the chicken in a bowl and sprinkle over the flour and some seasoning mix together
    2. Heat half the oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat, brown the chicken for 5 mins until golden, do this in batches, remove and set aside
    3. Add the remaining oil to the pan and fry the mushrooms for 3-5 mins until nearly cooked though, return the chicken and any jiuces to the pan, pour over the wine and simmer for 2 mins, then stir in the creme fraiche mustard and most of the tarragon, bring to the boil and simmer for 5 mins or until chicken is cooked through
    4. Stir in the spinach and check the seasoning, sprinkle over the remaining tarragon, and serve with crusty bread to mop up the juices
