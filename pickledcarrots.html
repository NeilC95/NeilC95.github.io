Pickled Carrots
Ingredients

  • 1 tsp. black peppercorns
  • 1 tsp. crushed chilli flakes (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp. allspice berries
  • 240 mL rice vinegar
  • 240 mL water
  • 70 g caster sugar
  • 1 tbsp. salt
  • 450 g carrots, peeled and cut into sticks
  • 2 cloves garlic

Method
  1. In a small pot over medium-low heat, toast peppercorns, chilli flakes, and allspice until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes. Add in vinegar, water, sugar, and salt; increase heat to medium-high and bring to a boil. Once sugar is completely dissolved, remove from heat.
  2. Pack carrot sticks and garlic into a sterilised glass jar (or two pint-sized glass jars) and pour over vinegar solution. Seal jar, refrigerate, and let sit at least 12 hours before serving.
