Elderflower and Lemon Cheesecake
Ingredients

Shopping list

For the Base

  • 200 g shortbread biscuits
  • 50 g unsalted butter, melted
  • zest of 1 lemon

    • For the Filling

  • 400 g soft curd cheese
  • 150 g caster sugar
  • 200 ml crème fraîche
  • zest of 1 lemon
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 3 tbsp. elderflower cordial

    • Method
    1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (160°C fan) mark 4. Line the base of a 900g loaf tin with baking parchment.
    2. To make the base, blitz the biscuits in a food processor to make evenly sized crumbs. Tip into a bowl and stir in the butter and lemon zest. Press the mixture into the base of the loaf tin and bake for 10-12min until firm. Lower the oven temperature to 140°C (120°C fan) mark 1.
    3. Beat the curd cheese with the sugar, crème fraîche and lemon zest. Beat in the eggs, one by one, until smooth followed by the cordial. Pour onto the cooked base. Cook for about 50min to 1hr until just set. Cool on a wire rack, then chill.
