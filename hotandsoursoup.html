Hot and Sour Soup
Ingredients

Shopping List

  • 1 tbsp ground nut oil
  • 2.5cm piece of fresh root ginger, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 2 handfuls fresh shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 red chilli, de-seeded, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp Shaoxing rice wine or dry sherry
  • 700ml/1 pint vegetable stock
  • 220g canned bamboo shoots drained and rinsed
  • 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chinese black rice wine or balsamic vinegar
  • pinch of ground white pepper
  • 1 tbsp cornflour mixed with 2 tbsp cold water

    • Method
    1. for the soup base heat the oil in a wok, then fry the ginger and shiitake mushrooms for 2-3 minutes, or until softened.
    2. Add the chilli, Shaoxing rice wine, vergetable stock, bamboo shoots and the seasonings.
    3. Bring to the boil, then add the cornflour paste, stirring well, until thickened.
    4. Keep warm over low heat
