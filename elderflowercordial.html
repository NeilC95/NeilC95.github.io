Elderflower Cordial
Ingredients

  • 1 kg Granulated sugar
  • 1 liter boiling water
  • 50 g citric acid or juice 2 large lemons
  • zest of 2 large lemons
  • 15 elderflower heads, stalks removedelderflower heads, stalks removed

Method
  1. Put the sugar in a bucket or basin with the boiling water and stir to dissolve. Add the citric acid or lemon juice and the lemon zest.
  2. Shake the elderflowers to remove any insects and add the flowers to the sugar syrup. Cover and leave to stand for 1-2 days, stirring morning and night.
  3. Strain the elderflower cordial through muslin and decant into sterilised bottles. Itâ€™s now ready to use.
