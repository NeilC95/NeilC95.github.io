Chinese Pancakes
Ingredients

    Shopping List

  • 450g plain flour
  • 250ml Boiling Water
  • 85ml cold water
  • 1 tsp sesame Oil

Method
  1. Add the boiling water to the flour and mix well, add the cold water and then knead the dough thoroughly until smooth and the cover and rest for 15 mins.
  2. Working on a floured board divide into 2 and roll into a sausage shape 2 inches thick, but into 1 inch lengths (altogether 40 pieces) Flatten each piece with the hand into pancake shapes, keeping the cut sides on the top and bottom, lightly brush with oil and stick the pancakes together oiled side on the inside and roll out until about 13cm in diameter
  3. Place a deep frying pan on a medium heat, cook pancakes on one side until bubbles rise (about 20 seconds), then turn over and cook on the other side for about 10 seconds, until light brown, remove from pan and separate the pancakes quickly by pulling them apart
  4. Stack the seperated pancakes oiled side up and cover with grease proof paper
  5. Serve folded into quarters
