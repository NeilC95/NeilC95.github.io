German Kartoffelpuffer (Potato Cakes)
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • 1 pound potatoes (about 2 medium or 3 small russets, or any variety of starchy potato)
  • 1/2 medium onion
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt (plus more to taste)
  • Ground pepper (to taste)
  • Ground nutmeg (to taste)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil (for frying)
  • sour cream (optional)

    • Method
    1. Wash, peel, and grate the potatoes.
    2. Place the grated potatoes in a clean dishcloth or cheesecloth and squeeze as much of the liquid as you can out into a bowl.
    3. Let the liquid stand a few minutes then carefully drain, leaving the potato starch at the bottom of the bowl.
    4. Add the potatoes back to the starch.
    5. Add salt, pepper, nutmeg, and egg. Mix thoroughly.
    6. Grate the onion over the potatoes.
    7. Heat the oil in a large frying pan. You can use a nonstick pan and just a few drops of oil if you wish, but for best results, use 1/8 to 1/4 inch of oil.
    8. Drop about 1/2 cup of potato mixture into hot oil and flatten with the back of a spoon. Fry 3 to 5 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.
    9. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with applesauce and or sour cream.
