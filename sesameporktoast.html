Sesame Pork Toast
Ingredients

    Shopping List

  • 150g Pork mince
  • 1 egg white
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of freshly ground pepper
  • 2 tbsp Cornflour
  • 2 thin slices of white bread
  • 75g sesame seeds
  • 600ml vegetable oil

Prep
  1. Cut the crusts off the bread
  2. Lightly beat the egg white
Method
  1. In a bowl mix together the pork mince, egg white, salt, pepper and cornflour
  2. spread the mixture on the bread slices
  3. Place the sesame seeds onto a flat plate, Press down on the bread slices pork side down until thickly coated with seeds
  4. Heat the oil in a wok, until a cube of bread brownd in about 20 seconds
  5. Carefully lower in pork toasts, spread side down and deep fry for 5 mins, keeping them immersed
  6. Lift out and drain on absorbant paper towels
  7. cut into triangles and serve
