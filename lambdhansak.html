Ingredients

    Tyga Pots

    • Ginger and Garlic Paste
    • Tyga Masala No.6
    • Tyga Masala No.2
    • Tyga Masala No.14

    Shopping List

  • 750g diced leg of Lamb
  • 3 Onions
  • 3 tsp Vegtable Oil
  • 1 tbsp Tomato Puree
  • 100g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 75g red split lentils
  • 1 tbsp Honey or sugar
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 300g rice

Prep
  1. Preheat oven to 180 deg C
  2. Remove all excess fat from diced Lamb
  3. Finely dice the onions
Method
  1. In a casserole dish with a lid, heat the oil and add the onions and ginger and garlic paste, stir continuously, on a high heat for 4 miniutes
  2. Add Tyga Masala No. 6 and the lamb, cook until meat is browned
  3. Add 750ml water and bring to the boil
  4. Add Tyge Masala No. 14 (1/2 pot for medium and while pot for hot), tomato puree, chopped tomatoes, lentils, honey, lemon and salt, stir well and cover
  5. Place in the oven for 1 and 1/2 to 2 hours until lamb is tender, check occasionally and add a little water to the sauce if needed throughout cooking
  6. Sprinkle with coriander (if using)
Rice
  • Add rice to 350ml of cold water in a saucepan, cover then bring to the boil and simmer for 3 minutes, remove from the heat and leave covered for 45 minutes for the perfect rice
Serve
  • Serve with rice and Cumin Potatoes
