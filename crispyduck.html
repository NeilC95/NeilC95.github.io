Crispy Duck
Ingredients

    Shopping List

  • 1 Duck about 1.5kg
  • 1.2 lts chicken stock
  • 8 tsp sugar
  • 4 slices of root ginger
  • 8 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp dry sherry
  • 6 peiced of star anise
  • 1 tbsp Szechuan Peppercorns
  • 1.2 litres oil

Prep
  1. Cut the duck in half down the breast bone and back bone
Method
  1. Heat all the ingredients except the oil in a pan, Place Duck in the pan and simmer for 3 hours
  2. Remove duck from pan and cool
  3. When ready to serve heat oil in a wok to 180 C, deep fry the duck until golden brown and crisp, drain on paper towels and shred as the table
Serve
  1. Serve with shredded cucumber and spring onions, hoisin sauce and pancakes
