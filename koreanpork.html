Korean Pork
Ingredients

  • Pork Chops
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic minced
  • 1 tsp Sesame Oil
  • 1 tsp Ginger Minced
  • 2 tsp Sriracha Sauce
  • black pepper to taste

Method
  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. whisk together soy, honey, garlic, ginger, sesame oil and sriracha sauce, Pour ove pork Chops and marinade for 20 mins
  3. Heat the Olive Oil in large pan, Add the pork chops without marinade, cook for 5 mins on the first side, then flip and add the remaning marinade, and cook for 5 mins
  4. Place Pan in the oven and roast for 10 mins, or until Pork is cooked though
