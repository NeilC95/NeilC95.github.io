Ingredients

    Tyga Pots

    • Ginger and Garlic Paste
    • Tyga Masala No.26
    • Tyga Masala No.23
    • Tyga Masala No.5 (use only 6 x cardomom pods)
    • Tyga Masala No.7
    • Kashmiri Chilli

    Shopping List

  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large Onions
  • 3 tsp Vegtable Oil
  • 600g Chicken
  • 1 handful of coriander
  • 2 sprigs of mint
  • 1 or 2 green chillis (optional)
  • 100ml coconut milk

Prep
  1. Finely dice the onions
  2. chop the chicken into bite size chunks
  3. grind the Cardomom pods if you dont want them left whole
  4. blend in a liquidiser, coriander, mint, chillis and 100ml water
Method
  1. in a casserole dish or frying pan with a lid heat 3 tbsp oil, add the onions, ginger and garlic paste, 6 x cardomom pods and 1 tsp salt cook for 4-5 mins stirring continually un til the onions are soft
  2. Add Tyga Masala No. 26, Tyga Masala No. 23, Tyga Masala No. 7 and Kashmiri chilli (1/2 tsp mild, 1 tsp medium, 2 tsp hot) mix well and cook for 1 minute stirring continuously
  3. Add the blended coreander mix and 200ml water and simmer for 5 minutes
  4. Add the chicken, mix well bring to the boil, cover and simmer for around 10-15 minutes or until the chicken is cooked
  5. stir through coconut milk and serve
Serve
  • Serve with roti or naan and Cumin Potatoes
