2 Egg Pancakes
Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 80g Flour
  • 90g milk
  • 1 flat tsp baking powder
  • 2/3 tsp salt

Method
  1. Seperate eggs into 2 bowls
  2. to the yolks add milk, flour, baking powder and salt
  3. mix
  4. beat egg whites until stiff peaks
  5. gently fold beaten egg whites into batter
  6. Cook !!
