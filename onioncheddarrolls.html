Onion and CHeddar Rolls
Ingredients

Shopping list

  • 250g strong white flour
  • 250g strong wheat flour
  • 10g dried yeast
  • 10g salt
  • 300g water
  • 80g spring onion
  • 80g cheddar cheese

    • Method
    1. Make the dough by weighing flour into large mixing bowl and then adding yeast to one side and salt to the other, make a well and pour water into the well.
    2. Using your hands form a dough, when the dough has formed tip out on to counter
    3. Knead for 10 mins until smooth and elastic, place in bowl cover and leave to rise until doubled in size
    4. Heat oven to 220 deg C, Knock back dough, and roll to rectangle 45 x 30 cm approx
    5. Spread spring onions and cheddar over the top leaving 2cm border along bottom edge brush the border with melted butter and roll up pinching to seal
    6. Cut into 12 slices, and arrange on backing sheet
    7. Cover and leave to prove for 30 mins
    8. Bake in oven for 17-20 mins or until golden
