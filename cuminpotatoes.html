Ingredients

    Tyga Pots

    • Tyga Masala No.3

    Shopping List

  • 750g New Potatoes
  • 2 tsp Vegtable Oil
  • 1 pinch salt

Prep
  1. Finely slice the new potatoes, food processor or slicer on grater is easiest
Method
  1. Heat oil in frying pan, add Tyge Masala No. 3 and cook for 2 minutes
  2. Add potatoes and salt, fry for 20-25 minutes stirring occasionally, until the potatoes are cooked, add a little water if the potatoes start to stick to the pan
Options
  • Sweet potatoes can be used instead of new potatoes
