Tomato Soup
Ingredients

  • 1-1.25 kg Tomatoes
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 Small Carrot
  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 2 Squirts Tom Puree
  • 1 good pinch of sugar
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 pints hot vegtable stock or boullion

Prep
  1. Chop tomatoes into quarters or smaller, remove hard core if needed
  2. Peel and Dice Onion and Carrot
Method
  1. Heat oil in pan and when hot add oinion and carrot, cook gently until soft and faintly coloured
  2. holding the tube over the pan squirt in abtou 2 Tbsp of Tom Puree, stir it around so all the vegtables go red.
  3. Shoot the tomatoes in off the chopping board sprinkle in a good pinch of sugar and grind in a little black pepper
  4. Stir to mix everything together, put the lid on the pan dnd let the tomatoes stew over low heat for 10 mins, until they shrink down in the pan and their juices flow nicely, from time to time shake the pan to keep things mixed well
  5. Slowly pour in the 2 pints of hot stock, stirring at the same timeto mix it with the vegtables, turn up the heat to high to get the mixture to boiling point, then turn the heat back down and put the lid on the pan
  6. cook gently for 25 mins stirring a couple of times, at the end of the cooking the tomatoes will look very broken down and slushy
  7. Remove the lid from the pan take out the bay leaf pieces and throw them away, then either ladle the soup into blender or use stick blender to blitz the soup until smooth
  8. Soup can be gently reheated now to eat or frozen for up to three months. Defrost before re-heating
Serve
  • Serve with Toasted Pitta bread, salsa and green salad
