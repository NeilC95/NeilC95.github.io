Ingredients

    Tyga Pots

    • Kashmiri Chilli
    • Tyga Masala No.10

    Shopping List

  • 600g potato
  • 4 tbsp veg oil

Prep
  1. preheat oven to 220 deg C
  2. peel and cut potatoes into half (or quarters if large)
Method
  1. Bring a pan of water to the boil and cook the potatoes for 5 mins, drain and shake vigerously in the pan to roughen up the edges
  2. meanwhile mix Tyga masala no. 10 and Kashmiri Chilli (1/4 tsp for mild, 1/2 tsp for medium or 1 tsp for hot) with the oil
  3. place the spice and oil mix in a roasting tin, and heat up in the oven
  4. When the oil is hot carefully place the potatoes in the roasting tin tossing them in the hot oil
  5. Roast for 40-45 minutes, or until crisp and golden, tossing a couple of times during cooking
